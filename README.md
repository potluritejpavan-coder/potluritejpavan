# Smart Campus Navigator

## Lost & Found

The Lost & Found section assists students and visitors in retrieving lost belongings. You can report a lost item by filling out the online form or visiting the dedicated Lost & Found office.

### How to report a lost item:
1. Fill out the lost item report form.
2. Provide a detailed description of the item.
3. Include your contact information.


## Parking

This section provides information regarding parking availability on campus, including designated parking zones and regulations. 

### Parking Areas:
- **Visitor Parking:** Located at the main entrance, open 24/7.
- **Student Parking:** Available on the east side of the campus, requires a permit.
- **Handicapped Parking:** Clearly marked spaces near all major buildings.

### Parking Regulations:
- Please adhere to the posted signs.
- Do not park in areas marked as no parking.


## Restrooms

Locating restrooms can be a challenge, so we've made it easier with clear signage and maps.

### Restroom Locations:
- **Main Building:** All floors include restroom facilities.
- **Library:** Restrooms located near the main entrance.
- **Cafeteria:** Restrooms available next to the dining area.

### Accessibility:
- All restrooms are equipped to accommodate individuals with disabilities.

## Conclusion

The Smart Campus Navigator aims to enhance your campus experience by providing essential information at your fingertips.
